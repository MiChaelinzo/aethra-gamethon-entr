## ğŸ¥ Production Tips

### Recording Gameplay

**Software Options:**
- **OBS Studio** (Free, Professional) - Recommended
- **Windows Game Bar** (Built-in on Windows)
- **Mac QuickTime** (Built-in on Mac)
- **Loom** (Web-based, easy)

**Settings:**
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps (30fps minimum)
- **Bitrate:** 8000-12000 kbps for high quality
- **Audio:** Record separate microphone track

**Recording Tips:**
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Disable notifications
- Use full-screen mode (F11)
- Practice gameplay before recording
- Record 3x more footage than you need
- Get multiple takes of each segment

---

### Voiceover Recording

**Equipment:**
- USB microphone (Blue Yeti, Audio-Technica ATR2100)
- Or quality headset mic
- Quiet room with minimal echo
- Phone voice memos as backup

**Recording Tips:**
- Write script first, practice reading
- Speak clearly and enthusiastically
- Leave pauses between sentences for editing
- Record in segments (easier to fix mistakes)
- Do multiple takes of each line
- Keep consistent distance from mic

**Voice Direction:**
- Energetic but not over-the-top
- Clear enunciation
- Vary your tone to maintain interest
- Emphasize key words (renewable, climate, unique)
- Smile while talking (it changes voice quality!)

---

### Editing

**Free Software:**
- **DaVinci Resolve** (Professional, free version excellent)
- **iMovie** (Mac only, user-friendly)
- **Shotcut** (Cross-platform, open source)

**Paid Software:**
- **Adobe Premiere Pro** (Industry standard)
- **Final Cut Pro** (Mac only, very powerful)
- **Camtasia** (Screencasting focused)

**Editing Tips:**
- Cut out all dead air and mistakes
- Sync voiceover perfectly with visuals
- Add text overlays for emphasis
- Use transitions sparingly (cuts are fine!)
- Color correct gameplay if needed
- Add subtle background music

---

### Music Selection

**Free Music Sources:**
- YouTube Audio Library
- Free Music Archive
- Incompetech
- Bensound

**What to Look For:**
- Upbeat, positive tone
- Not too distracting
- Matches energy of narration
- Royalty-free / Creative Commons
- No vocals (competes with voiceover)

**Volume Levels:**
- Voiceover: 100% (primary)
- Music: 15-25% (background)
- Game sounds: 30-40% (accent)

---

### Text & Graphics

**On-Screen Text Guidelines:**
- Large, readable font (minimum 48pt)
- High contrast with background
- Stay on screen 2-3 seconds minimum
- Animate in/out smoothly
- Use consistent style throughout

**Suggested Fonts:**
- **Bold Sans-Serif:** Montserrat, Raleway, Open Sans
- **Match Game:** Space Grotesk (your game's font!)
- Avoid: Decorative fonts, thin weights, ALL CAPS paragraphs

**Graphics to Include:**
- Feature callouts (circles, arrows)
- Statistics overlays
- Logo/branding
- Social media handles
- URL prominently at end
