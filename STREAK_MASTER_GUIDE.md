## Common Questions

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: Your streak resets to 0, but you can start building a new streak immediately with the next day's challenge.

**Q: Can I complete the same challenge multiple times?**
A: You can only complete each daily challenge once. Replaying it won't increase your streak.

**Q: What time do challenges reset?**
A: Daily challenges reset at midnight GMT (Greenwich Mean Time).

**Q: Does completing regular levels count?**
A: No, only daily challenges count toward your streak. Regular levels and tournaments don't affect it.

**Q: What if I complete a challenge on Day 8, 9, 10, etc.?**
A: Keep going! The badge is earned at 7 days, but maintaining longer streaks is impressive and shows dedication.

**Q: Can I lose the badge after earning it?**
A: No! Once earned, the Streak Master badge is yours forever, even if your streak breaks later.
